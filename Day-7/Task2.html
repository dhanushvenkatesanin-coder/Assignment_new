How to cook pineapple kesari

step-by-step

  1. Prepare ingredients: rava, sugar, pineapple, ghee, water,cashew nut,dry grapes,cardamom.
  2. Roast rava in ghee for 5 minutes.
  3. Fry cashew nut,dry grapes,cardamom.
  4. boil water with pineapple until it get soft.
  5. Boil water with sugar and cardamom.
  6. Add saffron for color and flavor.
  7. Add roasted rava slowly, mix well.
  8. Cook until thick and rava is soft.
  9. Add ghee, fried cashew nut,dry grapes,cardamom; mix.
  10. now pineapple kesari is ready to eat.
